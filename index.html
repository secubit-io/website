⬡Compliance

HSM FIPS 140-3

Hardware Security Modules certified to the highest federal standard. Private keys never leave the secure enclave.

◈Security

Distributed MPC

Multi-Party Computation splits the key into shares — one secured in the HSM, one accessible to the user. No single signing party ever holds a complete key.

◉Auth

PassKey & Biometric

FIDO2/WebAuthn standard replaces passwords. PassKey is used for transaction authorization and MPC key encryption — bound to biometric verification.

▤Control

Policy Engine

Define transaction rules, spending limits, whitelists, and multi-sig approval workflows at the wallet level in HSM.

⊕Flexibility

Custodial & Non-Custodial

Operate in fully-managed custodial mode or retain key sovereignty with non-custodial infrastructure under your control.

⌘Integration

API-First

Simple RESTful API with SDK and comprehensive documentation. Integrate in hours, not months.
